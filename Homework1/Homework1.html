The Biology of Cancer

The Hallmarks of Cancer

  1. Self-sufficiency of growth signals.
  2. Loss of response to anti-growth signals.
  3. Resistance to apoptosis.
  4. Limitless replication potential.
  5. Sustained angiogenesis.
  6. Tissue invasion and metastasis.
  7. Deregulating cellular genetics.
  8. Genome instability and mutation.
  9. Avoiding immune destruction.
  10. Tumor-promoting inflammation.
   

Current Treatment Methods

  • Surgical Removal
  • Chemotherapy
  • Radiotherapy
  • Targeted and Immunotherapy (see below!)
 

Immunotherapy

The prevention or treatment of disease with substances that stimulate the immune response.
